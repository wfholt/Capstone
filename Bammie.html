Bammie Davis Jr. - FUN


Nashville native Brandon Holt, aka Bammie Davis Jr. just dropped a hot new single titled "FUN" that most definitely evokes the spirit of the word. Known as a child prodigy on drums, many Nashvillians may remember Holt as the ankle biting drummer from the now defunct Biscuits & Gravy band. Now under the moniker Bammie Davis Jr., the kid is all grown up and ready to let the woreld know he's more than just a one trick pony. “I’ve played music since I was six,” he says, “and I always wrote songs just for myself. I did it for fun, posting songs on Tumblr, Bandcamp, and Soundcloud. I didn’t think anyone would notice. Then when I saw what was possible with Kat Milk Blu, I figured it was time I did my thing for real.” If you aren't familiar with Kat Milk Blu, this is the band that Bammie Davis Jr. is currently playing with. Their unique blend of Cali surf vibes and soulful rhythm sections will make you think of No Doubt mixed with The Internet. With such an open minded outfit as his main gig, it's no surprise that his original sounds span a broad spectrum of influences and sounds.

On July 27th, 2018, Kat Milk Blu released their 12-song debut album entitled, ‘Breakfast’. They are a live five-piece rock n’ roll/funk n’ soul band that holds true to the spirit of the 70’s with modern-pop sensibility. Now following this release up with his first solo single, "Fun" produced by his older brother D. Holt, Bammie Davis Jr. is poised to let the world know how he gets down.



You can also catch Bammie Davis Jr. featured on tracks by Jamiah Hudson, Mines Music and on Tim Gent's latest single "Pain Pills", which features a Davis Jr. penned hook. "I work with the people around me. If we have vibe, we can do something, ya know?" From the sound of things, there is plenty of vibe to go around as the young multi-talented musician's list of features continues to grow.
